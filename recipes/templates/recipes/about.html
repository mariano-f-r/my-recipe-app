About

Why did you make this?

I created this app under the guise of a benign recipe sharing app, when in actually it is an evil recipe sharing app, that will allow me to steal your recipes for when I go to college. Also, I was bored.

What technologies did you use?

I used Django as a backend, due to its simplicity, power, and the fact that Python is my favorite programming language. On the frontend, I employed Bulma, for sheer ease of use, the fact that it's open source, and it's execellent documentation. Would I recommend these 2? Yes. Yes I would.

Is it Open Source?

Yes, it is, and you WILL be able to contribute to it on GitHub.
